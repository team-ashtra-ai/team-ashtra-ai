Our process

Start sharp. Build strong. Keep moving.

Strong outcomes come from clarity, not guesswork. The process is built to keep things focused from the start, sharp through the build, and ready for what comes next.

01
Step 1

Discovery Consultation

This is where we find the gap. We look at where the company stands now, what feels weak, what is missing, and what is stopping the brand from landing the way it should.

  • Discovery conversation around the company, the offer, and the audience.
  • Review of what feels dated, unclear, weak, or behind.
  • Identification of what is costing trust, attention, or momentum.
  • Early direction on whether the right move is a new build, redesign, rebuild, or something more focused.

Outcome: A clearer view of the problem and a stronger starting point for everything that follows.

Find the gap first.

02
Step 2

Set the Direction

Once the gap is clear, the direction gets locked in. This is where the company gets clearer on what it needs to say, how it needs to feel, and what level it needs to rise to.

  • Clear direction for the message and the overall tone.
  • Priorities for what the site needs to communicate first.
  • Stronger understanding of what the brand should feel like online.
  • Alignment around what matters most before the build starts.

Outcome: A stronger plan, a clearer standard, and a much cleaner route into the build.

Lock the direction.

03
Step 3

Build the Site

This is where the new presence takes shape. The core website gets built with the right pages, the right structure, and the right feel.

  • Build of the core website or key pages.
  • Strong structure across the main sections.
  • Clear flow from first impression to action.
  • Mobile-first thinking so the site holds up properly across devices.

Outcome: A stronger website that looks ready, feels credible, and gives the company a serious digital base.

Build the core properly.

04
Step 4

Refine the Finish

Once the main build is in place, the finish gets sharpened. This is where the rough edges come off and the whole thing starts to feel more deliberate, more premium, and more complete.

  • Refinement of visuals, spacing, and overall polish.
  • Stronger flow across key sections and user paths.
  • Cleaner balance between message, design, and trust.
  • Final tightening so the whole experience feels intentional rather than pieced together.

Outcome: A finished presence that feels sharper, cleaner, and far more ready to carry weight.

Sharpen the edge.

05
Step 5

Optimise the Site

This is where the site starts working harder behind the scenes. Once the core presence is built and refined, the focus moves to the technical layer that supports growth, visibility, lead flow, and smarter decisions.

  • SEO foundations and search-friendly setup.
  • Google Analytics and core tracking configuration.
  • CRM and form connections where needed.
  • Stronger setup for visibility, measurement, and future optimisation.

Outcome: A site that does more than look good - it supports search, tracking, lead flow, and real business movement.

Turn the engine on.

06
Step 6

Support the Next Stage

Some companies need the build and launch. Others need ongoing support after that. This step is for the brands that want the presence to stay sharp, keep pace, and continue improving as the company grows.

  • Ongoing updates and improvements.
  • New sections or pages as the company expands.
  • Refinements based on new priorities or feedback.
  • Longer-term alignment between the site and where the company is heading next.

Outcome: A digital presence that keeps moving with the company instead of falling behind again.

Keep the momentum.
