How it works

No drift. No drag. Momentum.

The process is designed to keep things clear, focused, and commercially strong from day one. Each stage strengthens the read, the build, and the technical layer behind it.

  • Discovery and Direction remove guesswork.
  • Build and Refinement turn the standard into something visible.
  • Optimisation and Support keep the presence useful after launch.

Every phase has a job. Every phase moves the project forward.

Phase 01

Clarify the gap before the build begins.

Discovery and Direction exist to remove guesswork. The aim is to understand what is weak now, what the company needs to communicate, and what standard the next version needs to reach.

01 Discovery Consultation

Find the gap first.

We look at where the company stands now, what feels weak, what is missing, and what is stopping the brand from landing the way it should.

  • Discovery conversation around the company, the offer, and the audience.
  • Review of what feels dated, unclear, weak, or behind.
  • Early direction on whether the right move is a new build, redesign, rebuild, or something more focused.

Outcome: a clearer view of the problem and a stronger starting point.

02 Direction

Lock the direction.

Once the gap is clear, the message, priorities, feel, and standard get locked in before the build starts.

  • Clear direction for the message and overall tone.
  • Priorities for what the site needs to communicate first.
  • A sharper sense of what needs to be shown, said, and emphasised.

Outcome: a stronger plan, a clearer standard, and a cleaner route into the build.

Phase 02

Build the core, then sharpen the edge.

The build stage is where the new presence becomes visible. Refinement is where it stops feeling assembled and starts feeling intentional.

03 Build

Build the core properly.

The new presence takes shape with the right pages, the right structure, and the right feel.

  • Build of the core website or key pages.
  • Clear flow from first impression to action.
  • Mobile-first thinking so the site holds up across devices.

Outcome: a stronger website that looks ready and gives the company a serious digital base.

04 Refinement

Sharpen the edge.

Once the main build is in place, the finish gets tightened until the whole thing feels more deliberate, more premium, and more complete.

  • Refinement of visuals, spacing, and polish.
  • Stronger flow across key sections and user paths.
  • Final tightening so the whole experience feels intentional rather than pieced together.

Outcome: a finished presence that feels sharper, cleaner, and far more ready to carry weight.

Phase 03

Turn the engine on and keep the momentum.

The launch is not the end of the job. Optimisation strengthens what happens behind the scenes, and support keeps the presence aligned as the business moves.

05 Optimisation

Turn the engine on.

This is where the site starts working harder behind the scenes for visibility, lead flow, and smarter decisions.

  • SEO foundations and search-friendly setup.
  • Google Analytics and core tracking configuration.
  • CRM, forms, integrations, performance improvements, and technical clean-up.

Outcome: a site that supports search, tracking, lead flow, and real business movement.

06 Support

Keep the momentum.

Some companies need the launch only. Others need the presence to keep moving as the business grows.

  • Ongoing updates and improvements.
  • New sections or pages as the company expands.
  • Longer-term alignment between the site and where the company is heading next.

Outcome: a digital presence that keeps moving with the company instead of falling behind again.
