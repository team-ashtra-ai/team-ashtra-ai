1. What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on a device when someone visits a website. They help websites function, remember preferences, and understand how the site is being used.

2. How ash-tra.com uses cookies

Cookies may be used for:

  • core website functionality
  • security
  • remembering preferences
  • analytics and performance measurement
  • understanding how visitors move through the site
  • improving future user experience

3. Types of cookies that may be used

Essential cookies
These help the site function properly.

Analytics cookies
These help measure usage and performance.

Preference cookies
These help remember settings or user choices.

Marketing cookies
These may be used in the future if marketing or remarketing tools are enabled.

4. Your choices

Where required, non-essential cookies should only be used after the user has made a clear choice. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings where applicable.

Please note that disabling some cookies may affect how parts of the site work.
