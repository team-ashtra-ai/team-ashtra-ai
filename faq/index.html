FAQ

Short answers before the rebuild starts.

The usual questions about fit, motion, mobile quality, SEO, portfolio privacy, and how projects typically begin.

What does ASH-TRA actually do?

ASH-TRA redesigns and rebuilds websites for brands that want a sharper, more cinematic, more premium digital presence.

Can the site feel more premium without turning into visual chaos?

Yes. That is the whole point. More atmosphere and stronger motion should still support clarity, not bury it.

Will it still work well on mobile?

It has to. The layouts, imagery, and motion are designed to stay strong on smaller screens, not collapse after desktop is done.

Can the site include richer animation, imagery, and space-inspired visuals?

Yes. Motion, stage effects, real space media, and cinematic scenes are all on the table as long as they stay controlled and purposeful.

Will the site still be SEO-friendly?

Yes. SEO is handled as structure: better metadata, page hierarchy, inter-page linking, semantics, and readable content flow.

Do you share client work publicly?

Not by default. The public portfolio stays as direction studies. Approved client work can be discussed privately when it helps the brief.

Who is the work best suited for?

Ambitious founders, agencies, consultants, AI teams, and premium service businesses that care about digital taste and perceived quality.

What kinds of projects are not a fit?

If the goal is the cheapest, fastest, most generic template possible, this is probably not the right direction.

Do I need a polished brief before reaching out?

No. A clear note about what feels weak now and what kind of presence you want next is enough.

How do quotes usually work?

Quotes depend on scope, level of polish, strategic depth, and whether the project is a redesign or a more complete rebuild.
