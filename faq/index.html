FAQ

Questions that usually come up before a premium redesign or rebuild.

What does ASH-TRA actually do?

ASH-TRA designs premium websites for brands that want a more cinematic, more current, more technically polished digital presence.

Can the site feel more premium without turning into visual chaos?

Yes. That is the point. The goal is stronger atmosphere, sharper design, and better perception without sacrificing clarity or control.

Will it still work well on mobile?

It has to. The site is designed mobile-first, not treated like a desktop layout that gets squeezed down later.

Can the site include animation and motion?

Yes. Motion is part of the premium feel, but it stays controlled, performant, and respectful of reduced-motion preferences.

Will the site still be SEO-friendly?

Yes. SEO is handled as part of the structure: metadata, hierarchy, semantics, clear information architecture, and readable page flow.

Do you share client portfolios publicly?

Not by default. Public examples are direction studies. Approved client work can be discussed selectively in private when it helps the brief.

Who is the work best suited for?

Ambitious founders, consultants, agencies, and modern service brands that care about design quality and how the business feels online.

What kinds of projects are not a fit?

If the only goal is the cheapest possible website or a fast generic template, this is probably not the right fit.

Do I need a fully formed brief before reaching out?

No. A short explanation of what feels weak now and what you want the new site to feel like is enough to start.

How do quotes usually work?

Quotes are shaped around the scope, the level of polish, the amount of strategy needed, and whether the work is a redesign or a deeper rebuild.
