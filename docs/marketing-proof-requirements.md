# Marketing Proof Requirements

The public site should keep getting stronger over time through real proof, not only polish.

## Priority Additions

- named case studies
- quantified outcomes
- client testimonials with role/company context
- recognizable partner or platform references
- before/after artifacts where permission allows
- delivery screenshots from the dashboard experience

## Content Gaps To Fill Later

- fuller showcase inventory beyond the initial sample proof page
- more explicit service-page proof for each offer category
- reusable metrics that can appear in hero/proof bands
- proof that supports the design-led website transformation positioning

## Editorial Standard

Every new proof item should answer at least one of these:

- Why should the buyer trust the team?
- What changed materially after the work?
- Why does the process feel more premium than a generic redesign?
