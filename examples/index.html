What the public examples are for

They prove visual judgment, not fake outcomes.

  • They show the level of hierarchy and atmosphere ASH-TRA is built to deliver.
  • They make the design standard visible before a conversation starts.
  • They keep the public site honest instead of padded with invented case study language.
What stays selective

Private client work is handled with more care.

  • Approved material can be discussed privately when it helps the brief.
  • Client confidentiality is treated as part of the studio standard.
  • The public site is not used as a dump for sensitive or unapproved work.

Typography with confidence

Oversized when needed, disciplined where it counts, and never left to default system choices.

Atmosphere that feels expensive

Depth, lighting, contrast, and media treatment doing real work for the first impression.

Motion that respects structure

Enough movement to feel premium, never so much that the site turns into noise or lag.

Layout that feels authored

More than stacked sections. The pacing should feel deliberate from top to bottom.
