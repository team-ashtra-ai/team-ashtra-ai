What the work includes

The visual lift only works when the foundations improve too.

A more premium site is the result of many things moving together: messaging, composition, contrast, motion, responsive behavior, and cleaner technical structure.

Visual hierarchy

Clearer page rhythm, stronger typography, cleaner section flow, and layouts that feel authored instead of assembled.

Motion with restraint

Reveal states, hover polish, scroll pacing, and transitions that add atmosphere without creating noise.

Mobile-first responsiveness

Designed to feel convincing on smaller screens from the start, not repaired after a desktop-first concept.

Search-ready structure

Semantics, metadata, accessible patterns, and cleaner information architecture that support visibility and trust.
